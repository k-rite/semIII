Analysis of recursive methods:

Algorithm:
    Decide param upto input's size
    algo basic operations 
    best/worst Case
    setup recurrence relation 
    solve the recurrence relation

Backward method for identifying the best case
M(n) = M(n-1)+1
M(n-1) = M(n-1-1) + 1
    =M(n-2)+2
M(n-2) = M(n-3)+3
M(n-3) = M(n-4)+4
    =m=(n-i) +i
    =m(n-n)+n
    =1+n
    =O(n)
