====================================
Divide and Conquer Technique
====================================
Divide: Divide into sub problems
Conquer: Solver every sub problem
Combine: Solved sub problems combined together
Eg: Binary Search,Merge Sort,Quick Sort
-----------------------------------
Relational Formula
    Break problems in sub problems and solve sub problems recursively
Stopping Condition
    Condition which stops the recursion
-----------------------------------
Merge Sort | Divide till sub list has 1 element only
